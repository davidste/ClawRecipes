# Trademark & Branding Policy (ClawRecipes)

This policy helps users understand what they *can* do under the software license, and what requires permission with respect to **names, logos, and branding**.

## Summary

- The **code** is licensed under Apache-2.0 (see `LICENSE`).
- The **name and branding** ("ClawRecipes", logos, and related marks) are **not** automatically licensed for reuse.

## You MAY

- Refer to the project by name for attribution and compatibility statements, e.g.:
  - "Built with ClawRecipes"
  - "Compatible with ClawRecipes"
  - "Forked from ClawRecipes"
- Use the name in factual descriptions and documentation.

## You MAY NOT (without written permission)

- Use "ClawRecipes" (or confusingly similar marks) as the name of your fork or derivative product.
- Use ClawRecipes logos/branding in a way that implies endorsement, sponsorship, or official status.
- Represent a modified build as an official ClawRecipes release.

## Recommended fork naming

If you fork this repository, please rename your distribution/package to avoid confusion.

## Contact

If you want permission to use the ClawRecipes name/logo in your distribution, contact the maintainers via the GitHub repository:
